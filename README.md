# Symoh-Thewizard-
My biography
Symoh Thewizard (born Nyetese Simon on 5 February 1995 in Mitete, Mawogola County, Masaka Region, Uganda) is a Ugandan Information Technology consultant, entrepreneur, software expert, educator, philanthropist, and the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Wizard Tech Hub, a technology company based in Makindye, Kampala. He earned a Diploma in Information Technology from Mutesa I Royal University and began his career working with organizations including the National Information Security System (NSIS) and the Electoral Commission of Uganda. Between 2016 and 2018, he worked in the Kingdom of Bahrain with WABAG International as a Wastewater Plant Operator, specializing in SCADA-based computerized systems and industrial automation. During his time abroad, he also gained recognition as a DJ and participated in entertainment events in Bahrain and the United Arab Emirates. His professional expertise and passion for technology have enabled him to travel and work in several countries, including Bahrain, the UAE, Qatar, Kenya, Ethiopia, Singapore, China, and Laos, where he gained international experience in information technology and digital innovation. Through Wizard Tech Hub, he provides ICT consultancy, software support, computer services, technology training, and digital solutions for individuals and businesses. Beyond technology, he is actively involved in philanthropy, youth empowerment, education, and community development initiatives across Uganda. A father, mentor, teacher, and local farmer, Symoh Thewizard is recognized for his commitment to technological advancement, entrepreneurship, and using digital skills to create opportunities and drive positive social transformation.
